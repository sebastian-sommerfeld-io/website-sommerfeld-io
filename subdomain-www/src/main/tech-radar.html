What is the Tech Radar?

The Tech Radar is a list of technologies, complemented by an assessment result, called ring assignment. I use four rings with the following semantics:

  • IN USE - Technologies I use for my development purposes or to serve my website of for anything tech-related. These technologies form the toolbox from which I try to tacke all my projects.
  • TRIAL - Technologies that I started to use in a single project or limited usecase. Mostly this means I am learning about this technology with some real issues and tasks and not just on a hello-world-level.
  • ASSESS - Technologies that are promising and have clear potential to invest some research and prototyping efforts in to see if it has impact. Mostly this means I am learning about this technology through its documentation or on a hello-world-level.
  • HOLD - Technologies not recommended to be used for new projects. Technologies I decided not to use for various reasons. In most cases there is an Architecture Desicion Record explaining this decision in detail.
